STRIKERS v3.3 — BULLPEN INTELLIGENCE

Adds:
- Three-day reliever workload tracking from MLB box scores
- Bullpen availability score from 20–100
- Fresh / Manageable / Taxed / Critical fatigue labels
- Likely unavailable and limited reliever detection
- Bullpen ERA and WHIP when MLB's relief split is available
- Bullpen weighting inside the prediction engine
- Matchup Predictor bullpen comparison card

The existing Live Game Tracker and Live Games Dashboard are preserved.

Changed files:
- services/bullpen.py (new)
- services/prediction.py
- web_api.py
- frontend/src/App.tsx
- frontend/src/App.css

Install by copying the Strikers2 folder over your existing Strikers2 folder and selecting Replace files in destination.
