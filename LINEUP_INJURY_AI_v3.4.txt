STRIKERS v3.4 — LINEUP, INJURY INTELLIGENCE + AI GAME ANALYST

Changed files:
- web_api.py
- services/lineup_injuries.py (new)
- frontend/src/App.tsx
- frontend/src/App.css

New behavior:
- Reads official MLB batting orders when available.
- Displays confirmed/projected lineup status and lineup strength.
- Reads official injured-list roster entries.
- Applies a capped lineup/injury adjustment (maximum 4 percentage points).
- Prevents injury data from overpowering the core prediction model.
- Generates a model-grounded AI Game Analyst report.
- Preserves Live Game Tracker, Live Games, Bullpen Intelligence, history, and performance pages.

Important limitations:
- Day-to-day injuries and late scratches may not appear in MLB's official injured-list feed.
- Lineups remain marked projected/unavailable until MLB publishes the batting order.
- The analyst is generated from Strikers inputs; it does not call a paid external AI API.
