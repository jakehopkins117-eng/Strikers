STRIKERS BETTING INTELLIGENCE v1

Added:
- Bet Quality Score from 0 to 100
- Model edge vs sportsbook implied probability
- Expected value calculation
- Fair American odds
- Full Kelly percentage for transparency
- Conservative quarter-Kelly suggested stake
- Stake cap of 2 units
- Best available value banner
- Automatic PASS on negative-EV prices

Staking convention:
- 1 unit is approximately 1% of bankroll
- Suggested units are rounded to 0.25u
- This is informational analysis, not a guarantee of profit

Odds remain user-entered. Strikers does not invent or scrape sportsbook prices in this release.
